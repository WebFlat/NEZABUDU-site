ETHICS OF FILLING IN THE QUESTIONNAIRE AND KEEPING A PROFILE

The administration of the site nezabudu.com sets itself the task of perpetuating the memory of the deceased, and also assisting in the preservation of photographs, various data about life, memories, burial place, etc. The administration of the site nezabudu.com.ua considers it the main task to preserve the bright memory of relatives, friends, public and other people. Therefore, the Administration of the site nezabudu.com.ua moderates the data that leaves the user filling out the questionnaire, for the presence of negative and insulting statements about the departed, in in accordance with the Privacy Policy and User Agreement. We advise you to adhere to the following principles, filling out questionnaires, in order to avoid blocking the User and data moderation:

1) Describe the deceased in a positive way, talk about interesting, significant events in his life;

2) Do not use expressions and words that defile the memory of the departed;

3) Answer the questionnaire as honestly and truthfully as possible;

4) If some information is not known for certain, ask relatives again, to whom the data is known and added to the questionnaire later;

5) Describe periods of life and events that do not contradict moral standards and do not offend the feelings of relatives, friends, acquaintances, etc.;

6) We know how difficult it is to talk about a departed loved one, but your revelations will make it is possible to write a real life story of the departed;

7) We want filling out questionnaires to become not just a formal action, but really immersed you in memories and enabled loved ones, descendants and the growing generation to better recognize the deceased and preserve the true history of the family, and as a result, it has become a real chronicle of the lives of many generations.

8) We propose to create as many questionnaires as possible, and add memories there as they emerge in memory, to keep as much information about their ancestors as possible.

In accordance with the User Agreement, we remind you that according to P. 2.2.1.1. nezabudu.com.ua will fall on the User who committed offensive or illegal actions, degrading or hurting the feelings of loved ones, etc. in accordance with the Legislation of Ukraine (clause 2.2.1.1). The User is personally responsible for the information provided to Third Parties. Reliability and veracity of the information provided, which the User indicates in the profiles of the Site The site administration does not check.

2.2.1.2. In the event that the Visitors and/or Users of the site discover unreliable, untrue, insulting information degrading the feelings and dignity of Visitors, Users and other people after receiving a complaint such information will be manually moderated by the Site Administration, and if the complaint is confirmed, will be removed immediately. Punishment for posting unreliable, untruthful, insulting information that degrades the feelings and dignity of Visitors, Users and other people is provided within the framework of legislation of Ukraine and will be sent to the User who has committed an illegal action).
